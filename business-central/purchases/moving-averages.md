# Moving Averages (Power BI report)

The _Moving Averages_ focuses on the monthly purchase amount and how it compares to the 30-day moving average to identify trends and measure the daily spending. 

This report is designed for the purchasing managers.

## What the report shows

The *Moving Averages* shows the purchase amount by fiscal month and how it compares over time to the 30-day moving average. The 30-day moving average smoothes out short-term flunctuation and helps with analysing the overall trends. It is calculated from the average of all purchases made in the last 30 days.

## How to use the Moving Averages report

The _Moving Averages_ report can be used in various ways, such as 
- **Identify Trends**: Identifies trends in purchase activities for a given fiscal period.
- **Budget Control**: Identifies the purchase amount month by month and tracks its difference comparing to previous fiscal periods.
