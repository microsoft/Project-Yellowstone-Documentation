# Daily Purchases (Power BI report)

The _Daily Purchases_ highlights the daily purchases throughout the fiscal year. Shows purchase amount by each week day. And detailed drillthrough to manage and track each purchasing activity within the organization. 

This report is designed for the purchasing team, particularly for purchasing managers and officers.

## What the report shows

The *Daily Purchases* reports provides a snapshot of purchasing activities within the organization.

## How to use the Daily Purchases report

The _Daily Purchases_ report can be used in various ways, such as 
- **Tracking Expenses**: Helps the purchasing team to track their daily spending for all purchasing activties.
- **Identifying Patterns & Trends**: Reveals purchasing patterns and trend, helping purchasing managers to make better decisions and control budget.
- **Procurement Planning**: Helps with forecasting expenses and tracking inventory to improve stock replenshiment and production.
